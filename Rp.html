Return & Refund Policy

We stand behind our products. If you're not completely satisfied, here's how to return them.

Return Eligibility

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. To be eligible:

  • Item must be unused and in original packaging
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Original proof of purchase is required

Note: Some items are final sale (software, gift cards, personalized products).

How to Return

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

1. Initiate Return

Log in to your account or use our return portal with your order number

2. Print Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label (US only)

3. Ship Item

Package securely and drop off at any carrier location

4. Get Refund

Refund processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return

Refund Process

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Standard returns: Refund includes item price minus shipping
  • Defective/wrong items: Full refund including shipping
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days after we receive your return

International customers: Refunds exclude original shipping and customs fees.

Exchanges

Need a different size or model? Here's how exchanges work:

  • Return the original item following our standard process
  • Place a new order for the replacement item
  • Email exchanges@stackly.com for price adjustments if the new item costs less

Tip: For fastest service, place your new order immediately after initiating the return.

Warranty Returns

Items still under warranty have special return options:

  • 2-year limited warranty on all electronics
  • No restocking fee for warranty claims
  • Advanced replacement available for registered products
  • Contact support@stackly.com for warranty service

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team can assist with any return questions or issues.
