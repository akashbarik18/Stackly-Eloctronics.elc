Our Story

Founded in 2025, Stackly began as a small startup with a big vision: to revolutionize the electronics industry. What started as a team of three engineers working in a garage has grown into a global leader in consumer electronics, with products in over 50 countries and a reputation for quality and innovation.

Our journey has been powered by a relentless focus on research and development, customer satisfaction, and sustainable manufacturing practices that minimize our environmental impact while maximizing performance.
